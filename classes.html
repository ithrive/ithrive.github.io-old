Class Levels

  • Level 1

    for beginners or those wanting to consolidate the foundations

  • Level 2

    provides a greater challenge. Participants are expected to have completed at least one Level 1 term

  • Level 3

    a strong workout at a faster past. Participants must have at least one year Pilates experience
