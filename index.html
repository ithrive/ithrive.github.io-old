Pilates is a system of body conditioning that helps you build long, supple, flexible strength. ithrive's matwork courses start slow and precise, to ensure you target the correct muscles, and build over the weeks and levels to challenge your core, your stability and balance for ever increasing strength and agility.

Meet your instructor, Laura Kavanagh. Laura is genuinely passionate about helping you move better. Her approach to Pilates is influenced not only by her own experience with overcoming injury, but also by more than 10 years experience as a remedial and soft tissue therapist. She combines knowledge of evidence-based best practise in exercise and rehabilitation with a flare for teaching that will have you moving like never before!

Why do people come to Pilates?

People come to Pilates with ithrive for many reasons, including:
  • to strengthen up and get back on their feet following injury
  • for postural awareness and improvement
  • for greater core strength to compliment their sport and other physical training
  • to look good and feel fantastic!
