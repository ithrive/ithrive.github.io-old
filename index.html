What is Pilates?

Pilates is a system of body conditioning that emphasises a flexible spine and strong core while fostering a deep mind-body awareness.

Founder, Joseph Pilates, defined it as "a way to completely and thoroughly unify the body, mind and spirit."

Meet Laura, your instructor

Laura is one of Canberra's leading Pilates instructors. She has an extensive background in soft tissue therapy, helping those with on-going physical injuries. Her deep understanding of the body underpins all of her practice ensuring you will get the most out of her classes.

Among her qualifications Laura has a Certificate IV in Pilates Matwork Instruction, an Advanced Diploma of Soft Tissue Therapy and a Certificate IV in Assessment and Training.
