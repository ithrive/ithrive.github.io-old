Welcome to ithrive Pilates matwork classes

ithrive is a boutique Pilates business offering small group mat-work classes with an emphasis on quality instruction and individualised attention.

What is Pilates?

Pilates is a system of body conditioning that emphasises spinal mobility and stability, posture and awareness.

Founder, Joseph Pilates, defined it as "a way to completely and thoroughly unify the body, mind and spirit." Through Pilates you will build a lean, supple strength, beautiful posture and a level of body awareness that will permeate your every movement with grace and fluidity and a natural resistance to injury.

In Pilates mat-work you use primarily your own body weight to provide resistance in a flowing sequence of exercises. A class will systematically mobilise your spine, then centre you, strengthen your core and postural muscles, then challenge you to integrate all the elements to finish with a body that feels lengthened, energised and balanced.

It begins gentle enough to be suitable for all - all age groups, all body types, all levels of conditioning, though with a four-tiered system of difficulty (from level one to level four) there is a class to suit everyone: from the most timid beginner to the most adventurous of body sculptors.

Is Pilates right for me?

Whether you are recovering from injury, need a postural overhaul or simply want to tone and strengthen, ithrive has a class for you.

Many people choose ithrive Pilates
To strengthen up and get back on their feet following injury
For greater postural awareness and improvement
To compliment their sport and other physical training
To stretch, strengthen, energise and feel fantastic!

Level 1 classes are designed to give you a good grounding in the basics. Discover:

  • Your core muscles and how to strengthen them
  • Neutral spine and the foundations of great posture
  • The art of breathing fully and well
  • How to mobilise your own spine
You will also begin to improve awareness around your own body, breaking down poor postural habits where necessary and strengthening up in areas of weakness or instability.

Level 2 classes build on the foundations learnt in level 1. You will begin to string exercises together in a flowing sequence to build greater strength and endurance.

The focus each week can vary and may include emphasis on a particular body area - say low back, shoulders, wrists, or knees; or a particular muscle group - hamstrings, back extensors, gluteals. The variations are endless - but your abs will ALWAYS get a great workout!
